Clovis Area Recreation

Jun 18 at 8 AM – Jun 21 at 1 PM

The 5th Annual Clovis PD Cops and Kids Sports Camp is a youth summer camp designed to teach 1st through 6th grader's basic sports skills, in a fun and safe environment. Cops coaching kids is our top priority, and over the course of the four day camp the activities will include: baseball/softball, basketball, indoor soccer, volleyball, obstacle course, and Friday Fun Day. The cost of the camp is $20 per participant. The camp runs from 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM on June 18, 2019 - June 21, 2019. The fee includes lunch and a T-Shirt.

Registration for the Cops & Kids Sports Camp will open on Monday, April 29, 2019, at 10:00 A.M. Spots are limited and this camp fills up extremely quickly.

The City of Fresno’s Active Transportation Plan recommends the development of 166 miles Class I Bike Paths throughout Fresno. Where to start? The Fresno Trails Network Expansion Feasibility Study will prioritize projects and identify the top five projects and develop for designs so that the City can seek funding for them. Old Town Clovis Events

Saturday, September 21, 2019 at 10 AM – 5 PM

Bitwise Events

Friday, June 7, 2019 at 5 PM – 7 PM

Bitwise Industries 700 Van Ness Ave, Fresno, California 93721

Black Leaders Organizing Change - BLOC Fresno and the Fresno Metro Black Chamber of Commerce (FMBCC) are taking over June’s Geek Night! Stop by on Friday, June 7 for a showcase of some of the Valley’s most talented youth entrepreneurs and a look at their innovative businesses. Plus, BLOC Fresno and FMBCC will be hosting a joint mixer for all attendees.

We’ll have free food and beer while it lasts. Plus, grab a drink at the full bar. Soda and water will be available for purchase.

Established in September 2016, BLOC Fresno is a network of African American professionals in the Central Valley aimed at creating engagement opportunities for our community. FMBCC is a locally-based membership organization advancing the interests of the greater African American community through advocacy, business opportunities, and economic development.
